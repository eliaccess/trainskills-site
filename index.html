What is TrainSkills?

TrainSkills is a mobile app for athletes and coaches in combat sports — wrestling, MMA, BJJ/grappling, judo, boxing, kickboxing, karate — and CrossFit. It replaces the paper training notebook: you log every training session and competition, and TrainSkills turns your history into insights and personalized coaching.

With TrainSkills you can:

  • Log every session — sport, duration, techniques worked, fitness level, RPE, injuries and notes, with voice dictation.
  • Track competitions match by match: results, victory types, points and rounds, rankings and medals.
  • See your statistics — training volume, split by sport, fitness trends, injuries, techniques, competition record.
  • Ask the AI coach for advice tailored to your own training history.
  • Share with your coach — athletes share sessions sport by sport; coaches follow their athletes' progress.
  • Attach photos and videos to your sessions.

Available in English, French and Serbian. Free account, and you can delete your account and all associated data at any time directly in the app (Profile → Delete account). Sign in with email, Apple or Google.
